The story behind the stories

It started with a real Camila.

Tudo começou com uma Camila a sério.

Camila & Co. began the way a lot of bedtime stories do — with a little girl who wanted just one more, and a parent who kept making them up. Camila is inspired by a real daughter, and every adventure is written so she (and kids like her) get to be the hero.

Because our family lives in two languages, so do the books. Each story is told in European Portuguese and English, side by side — made for bilingual families reading together.

— Feito com amor, para a Camila. 💛
